Our Services

From parts sourcing to doorstep delivery — we handle everything so you can focus on keeping your business running.

What We Do

Six Services. One Purpose. Keeping You Operational.

From a single replacement part to a complete fleet supply contract — we have the capability and expertise to deliver.

01

Parts Sourcing & Importation

We import directly from verified manufacturers and distributors across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East. Our procurement team identifies the best source for each part — balancing quality, availability, and cost.

  • Genuine OEM & quality aftermarket parts
  • Global supplier network
  • Part number lookup
  • Spec-based sourcing
02

Wholesale & Bulk Supply

Operating a garage, fleet, or construction firm? We support high-volume orders with competitive bulk pricing, consistent stock levels, and dedicated account management for repeat clients.

  • Volume pricing available
  • Fleet & contractor supply
  • Consistent stock levels
  • Account management
03

Technical Consultation

Not sure which part fits? Our experienced team offers honest, knowledgeable advice on part compatibility, brand comparisons, and the right specification for your application.

  • Part compatibility checks
  • Brand & grade comparisons
  • Spec identification
  • OEM vs aftermarket advice
04

Express Local Delivery

We deliver across Dar es Salaam and ship upcountry through reliable freight partners. Urgent orders are handled with priority dispatch — because we know downtime costs money.

  • Dar es Salaam delivery
  • Upcountry shipping via freight
  • Priority dispatch for urgent orders
  • Order tracking support
05

Custom Parts Ordering

Need a part that's hard to find locally? Give us the part number, model, or specification and we'll source it from our international network. No request is too specific.

  • Hard-to-find parts
  • Custom specification sourcing
  • Part number matching
  • International procurement
06

Quality Assurance

We inspect all items before dispatch. Our QA process checks parts against order specifications, packaging integrity, and supplier certification. You receive what you ordered — every time.

  • Pre-dispatch inspection
  • Specification verification
  • Supplier certification check
  • Packaging integrity review
How It Works

Simple Process. Fast Results.

Getting your parts is straightforward — just four steps from enquiry to delivery.

01

Send Your Enquiry

Call, email, or fill in our contact form with your part details — part number, vehicle model, or a description works fine.

02

We Source It

Our team checks stock, contacts our supplier network, and finds the best option for your needs within 24 hours.

03

Quote & Confirm

We send you a clear quote with pricing and availability. Once confirmed, your order is processed immediately.

04

Delivery to You

Parts are inspected, packed, and dispatched — across Dar es Salaam or upcountry via our freight partners.
